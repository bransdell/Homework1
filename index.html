About Me

I hail from the small town of Bristol, ME. I moved to Iowa over the summer of 2009 for what I thought would be a change of pace. Little did I know that Iowa was very similar to Maine other than the fact that there wasn't any ocean.

I took a year off from school after I graduated from High School so that I could save up some money for college tuition. US Cellular hired me as a Customer Service Representative and I worked there for the better part of a year. I saved up just enough money so that I could pay for my tuition and buy a new car. I've been broke ever since my car became my addiction.


Me in a Nutshell:
